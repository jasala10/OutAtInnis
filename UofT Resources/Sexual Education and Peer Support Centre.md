The University of Toronto Sexual Education and Peer Support Centre is a 100% not-for-profit, volunteer-run student service/student group at the University of Toronto, St. George campus. SEC provides free supplies like condoms, lube and other safer sex products as well as services like confidential, non-judgmental peer sex education support via the phone, one-on-one peer support, library and resource services, and residence and high school talks.

#### Services 

Sex Ed Presentations: 

- SEC offers free presentations to University of Toronto groups. Their standard presentation covers sex & gender, sexuality, consent & communication, STIs, and birth control. 
- [Form to request a Presentation](https://www.secutoronto.org/presentations)

Safer Sex Supplies: 

- SEC offers free safer sex supplies, menstrual products, and pregnancy tests for the UofT and general community. [Here is a catalogue of the supplies offered.](https://www.secutoronto.org/our-safer-sex-supplies)  
- You can either visit SEC at 230 College St, Room 326 for these or [place a bulk order via this form](https://www.secutoronto.org/safer-sex-supplies).

Peer Support Counselling: 

- SEC has peer volunteers available to answer questions students may have about sex, sexuality, sexual health, etc. in a welcoming, non-judgmental, sex-positive environment. They can also help point you to services, clinic, support groups and resources within UofT, the community at large, or online/in print. 
- Volunteers are available Monday – Friday from 11am – 5pm during the academic year (September – April) except on days the University is closed in Rm 326 of the Student Commons.

Library and Resources 

- You can [access the SEC Library Catalogue here](https://utsec.librarika.com/search/catalogs). This contains 800+ books and journals on sex, sexuality, and sexual health literature. Books can be loaned for a 2-week period. 
- SEC also has available print resources from sexual health organizations that student groups can access free of charge at the SEC office or order in bulk by emailing [utsec.information@gmail.com](mailto:utsec.information@gmail.com) . Bulk print resource request orders will be available for pick-up within 10-14 business days of the request being received.
